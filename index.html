FAQs

What is a hackathon?
A hackathon is a place where the only thing limiting what you build is your own creativity - we'll provide the rest. Stay up for 36 hours with the smartest people you'll ever meet, building the coolest things you have created, and chugging more of your favorite energy drink than you ever thought possible.

Who can come?
Any undergraduate or graduate students over the age of 18.

What should I bring?
Yourself, deodorant, an idea, a photo ID, your hacking setup, a change of clothes, toiletries, and optionally a sleeping bag (pillow and/or blanket). Note: There will be no showers at the event.

Do I sign up as a team or individual?
You will apply as a team. We will be personally reviewing each application and accepting on a team basis.

What does it cost?
Hack UCI is free for all admitted hackers. We provide meals, caffeine, swag, workspace, and WiFi for the hackathon; travel reimbursements & bussing is also be available for qualifying hackers. We're committed to making Hack UCI accessible!

Past Projects

Marvel Medical Dictionary

Jomo, Kaleb, Ryan, and Khalid built a mobile, augmented reality experience that allows children to learn about health topics from a superhero related to the searched topic. Imagine Spider-Man teaching about spider bites! They won Best Mobile AR Hack at Hack UCI 2018.

The Ultimate Water Heater

Matthew and Amarinder built a heating system and a mobile app to build an accessible water heater that has 90% energy efficiency and could save 5 gallons of water per average shower. They won Most Interdisciplinary Hack and Greatest Impact Hack at Hack UCI 2018.

Computertop Desk

Omar combined a webcam and projector mounted above a desk, along with a splash of OpenCV to locate all the pieces of paper and the keyboard, allowing users to set any piece of paper as a window for the computer. Omar won Most Creative Hack and Best Hardware Hack at Hack UCI 2018.
